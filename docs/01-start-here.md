# Start Here

## Primary Resource

- [GSAP Scroll](https://gsap.com/scroll/)

Use this as your reference for scroll-driven motion patterns.

## What To Do First

1. Create issue `Project 2 Master Checklist`.
2. Create all sub-issues listed in that master issue.
3. Build your project using the structure in [Project Structure Suggestion](04-project-structure-suggestion.md).
4. Track progress in your master issue as sub-issues are completed.
5. Verify your final submission against [Final Turn-In Spec](03-final-turn-in-spec.md).

## Rule Of Focus

- Complete `Required` items first.
- Do not add stretch effects until required items are stable.
